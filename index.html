About me

I am a post-doctoral researcher in the Digital Security group at the Radboud University in the Netherlands, after obtaining my PhD there under the supervision of Prof. Lejla Batina. I obtained the title Master of Advanced Study after Part III of the Mathematical Tripos at the University of Cambridge. I received my BSc in Mathematics at Utrecht University. During the summers of 2016, 2017 and 2018 I was an intern in the Security and Cryptography group at Microsoft Research.

My current research interests include:

  • Efficient and secure implementations of curve-based crypto,
  • (Hyper)elliptic-curve cryptography,
  • Isogeny-based cryptography.
