About me

I am a Crypto & Security architect at NXP Semiconductors in Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

Before that I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Digital Security group at the Radboud University in the Netherlands, and obtained my PhD there with Prof. Lejla Batina with my thesis titled Security on the Line: Modern Curve-based Cryptography. I completed Part III of the Mathematical Tripos at the University of Cambridge after receiving my BSc in Mathematics at Utrecht University. During the summers of 2016, 2017 and 2018 I was an intern in the Security and Cryptography group at Microsoft Research in Redmond.
